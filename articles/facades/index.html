Facades of Grandeur

by
Steve Thomas

POEM
Everyone keeps chattering about,
Not slowing down
To stay and see
the beautiful things
Life gives for free.

They work for unrelenting dreams,
To build a beautiful house
But they always forget
That a home is what
Happiness is really about.

They step over other people,
In their dreams to be rich
But they never see nor hear
The cries of those
Fallen in the ditch.

That in the venture of desires,
You turned to a fool.
