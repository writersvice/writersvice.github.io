Consequences

by
Steve Thomas

POEM
Life is a very twisted thing,
The things that grant us to live,
Are dying because of our lack of feeling,
Our concern for nature is nothing except a lie.

We ignore all the signs around us,
And continue to kill our most precious resource,
The resource of life; is cut down,
And all this is done without remorse.

The thing we call our mother is dying,
And yet we do not pick up arms,
Arms made of spades and gardening tools,
The smartest creatures have been reduced to fools.

What exactly is a plant?
The beginning of life and the giver of life,
The colour in the suns and stars,
The humble symbol of unearthly might.

We cut down trees and make paper,
And on the same paper we write,
The ways and methods of giving it life,
That is the widespread human plight.

Therefore I implore you all,
To pick up spades and gardening tools,
Or otherwise we are destined to fall,
And the smartest creatures would be reduced to fools.
